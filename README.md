- **What is the purpose of a Leaderboard**

The Leaderboard project aims to provide a seamless and user-friendly solution for tracking and displaying performance rankings, offering participants an enhanced competitive experience while encouraging continuous improvement. With its flexibility and adaptability, it can be readily integrated into various projects, enhancing their competitiveness and fostering community engagement.
